Common issues

The bot isn't responding to commands. Make sure the app is installed in your workspace. Visit the install page to add it.

I get "No match" for my search. Frinkiac and Morbotron search by subtitle text, not by visual content. Try searching for a quote or line of dialogue from the scene you're looking for.

GIFs are loading slowly. GIFs are generated on the fly by Frinkiac/Morbotron and can take a few seconds. Longer frame ranges will take longer to generate.
