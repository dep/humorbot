Privacy Policy

Humorbot takes your privacy seriously. Here's what you need to know.

What we collect

When you install Humorbot, Slack provides us with an OAuth token that allows the app to respond to slash commands in your workspace. We store this token so the app can function.

When you use a command, Slack sends us your username, team info, and the command text. We log this temporarily for debugging purposes. We do not store your messages, search history, or personal information permanently.

What we don't do

We don't sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. We don't read your Slack messages. We don't use tracking cookies. The app only activates when you explicitly invoke a slash command.

Third-party services

Your search queries are sent to Frinkiac and Morbotron to generate screenshots and GIFs. These services have their own privacy policies. The app is hosted on Heroku.

Data removal

Removing the app from your Slack workspace revokes the OAuth token. If you'd like your installation data removed from our database, open an issue on GitHub.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be noted on the project's GitHub page.
