Two shows. Two commands.

Powered by Frinkiac and Morbotron's massive screenshot databases.

The Simpsons

/frink

Search every episode of The Simpsons. Generate still images with caption overlays or animated GIFs.

Futurama

/morbo

Search every episode of Futurama. Same features — stills, text overlays, GIFs, and a GIF editor.

How it works

/frink dental plan Search and share the best match
/morbo gif shut up and take my money Generate an animated GIF
/frink images homer Browse multiple results and pick one
/frink steamed hams | aurora borealis Custom text overlay
